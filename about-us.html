Know about Keerthi Makeup Artistry

Keerthi Makeup Artistry brings passion and expertise to every brush stroke. With over 5 years of experience, Keerthi Reddy, a renowned makeup artist from Nellore, crafts stunning looks that enhance natural beauty. Trained in Fine Arts & Fashion, she has worked with clients across India, specializing in bridal, editorial, and event makeup.

After learning professional makeup lessons from Dubai and Delhi, she expanded her talents to numerous places like Canada, Oman, Lucknow, and more, mastering the art of transforming faces. Keerthi blends creativity with skill to create unique looks with makeup and plays with colors to enhance different styles and personalities.

This inspiring journey began with a passion for makeup, leading to specialized training in dramatic looks, which made Keerthi a favorite among brides and celebrities. Her meticulous techniques ensure a flawless finish, making every client feel confident and beautiful.
